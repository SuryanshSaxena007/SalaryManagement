Design notes
------------
- Server Component (app/page.tsx) parses DAL responses with Zod schemas
  (KpiSchema, ByCountryRowSchema, ByDepartmentRowSchema) before handing
  resolved (non-promise) data to <DashboardClient/>. Avoids hydration
  mismatch and keeps validation in the trust boundary.
- 'export const dynamic = "force-dynamic"' opts the route out of static
  prerender; next build does NOT hit the backend.
- 4 KPI grid: 1 col → 2 col (sm) → 4 col (xl). Cards: Total Headcount,
  Total Comp (USD), Avg Salary (USD), Countries. Sublabels show median,
  raw count, department/currency counts. Formatters from lib/format.ts —
  no inline number/currency formatting.
- Charts use Recharts <ResponsiveContainer> + <BarChart>. Colors use
  globals.css design tokens (--color-primary, --color-border,
  --color-muted-foreground, --color-popover, --radius-md). Sorted desc
  client-side before render.
- Tests pass direct props (no MSW round-trip). Recharts SVG render in
  jsdom unlocked by mocking ResponsiveContainer to cloneElement the
  single chart child with explicit width/height — actual chart math
  runs, SVG nodes exist, assertions hold.
